# k3s

## What is it?

k3s is a tool for running and managing containers.
It is a lightweight version of Kubernetes, designed to be simpler and suitable for personal computers and small environments.

## When should it be used?

k3s is commonly used for small projects, development and testing environments, or when running Kubernetes on a personal computer.

## Where can it be used?

k3s can run on personal machines, small servers, and even company environments due to its lightweight and easy-to-install nature.

## Why it should be used?

k3s is worth using because it is lightweight, requires fewer resources, and is easy to install.
It comes as a single installation instead of multiple components, making it much simpler and less heavy than standard Kubernetes.
